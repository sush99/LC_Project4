These are my favorite Pokemon

Sceptile

Sceptile is my #1 favorite pokemon, and one of the first pokemon that i've ever had. He the Grass type starter from the Ruby/Sapphire/Emerald games and what caused me to love grass type pokemon from then till now. Whenever he does his "Mega Evolution", he becomes the first Grass/Dragon type Pokemon, and one of 3 that have existed till now.

Lucario

Lucario is a Steel/Fighting type pokemon introduced in Diamond/Pearl. I loved his design from when I was a kid and still love it to this day. He has the ability to use special Fighting type moves, which was introduced in that generation of Pokemon games.

Torterra

Torterra is the Grass/Ground starter from Diamond/Pearl. He is probably the pokemon that I have used the most in all the years that i've played the series. His design is pretty interesting; as he evolves he gets more land on his back growing from a small bush to a blooming ecosystem.

Gallade

Gallade is a Psychic/Fighting type introduced in Diamond/Pearl/ I really liked this design as a kid because Gallade has swords for hands and that was the coolest thing to me as a kid. He seemed like a white knight type of character, which reminded me of arthurian legends whenever I first saw him.

Luxray

Luxray is an Electric type introduced in Diamond/Pearl. His design goes from a lion cub to a proper king of the jungle, and he just looks really cool to boot.

Staraptor

Staraptor is a Normal/Flying type introduced in Diamond/Pearl. Every generation of pokemon has thier version of a bird that you find early in the game. Of these, Staraptor is my favorite because it, and Toretrra, were able to stay alive long enough to help me beat the final boss of Pokemon Pearl. His design being a little "edgier" than most also appealed to 9-year old me as well.
